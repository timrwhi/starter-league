Tooltips

This is a live demo of the tooltip pattern. Hover over tooltip content herethis link, and a helpful bubble will appear above the link.

It even works on other elements, like buttons:

I kept running into problems with applying long tooltip text to a small element (e.g. a short word), where the tooltip would wrap awkwardly. Adding white-space: nowrap to the .tooltip span solved this problem, and now tooltip text can be as long as I want without wrapping.
